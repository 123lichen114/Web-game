In recent years, there have been many cases of missing persons on the earth, and they have lost contact with their relatives.

The husband who worked extra late did not return, the little girl who should have gone home after school disappeared, and the teacher did not enter the classroom when the bell rang.

Recently, there have been many cases of missing persons in Xijing city. The police have received a lot of help information from their relatives, but the geographical scope is too large and no progress has been made.

Doctor Liu, a doctor of mathematical physics, rushed home from the research room, ready to announce his scientific research achievements to his elderly and lame mother, only to find that his mother, who was supposed to be in a wheelchair, had disappeared, which made him very anxious. He rushed downstairs to ask the security. He learned that in the evening, a man wearing a mask and sunglasses had fierce eyes and walked upstairs through the gate. At noon, a helicopter flew over the top of the building. He never saw the mysterious man again.

He anxiously followed the places his mother might have visited to find clues and traces. Finally, he found a secret rocket launching pad, which frequently operated at night. He used the police force to investigate this place. According to the informant's intelligence, he learned that it was Doctor Hier, the leader of the "new universe" group. In order to collect evidence and rescue the missing, he decided to fight to the death and fly to a distant alien.
